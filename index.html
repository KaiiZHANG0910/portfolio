Integrated Studio Project 2026

EDIT: A Dialogue with Digital Memory

"What is truly real in this AI age? What if the messages, people, and experiences can all be generated and coded?"

The inspiration for "EDIT" comes from my personal diary. As a girl from Shanghai studying in Hong Kong, browsing through my diary often makes my past memories and the people I cherish feel like a distant dream.

This existential doubt was amplified after I experienced a scam in Hong Kong. It was an alarming wake-up call that forced me to confront the blurred lines of reality in our AI age.

While this initial thought was desperate, I found my answer: Emotions, reactions, and feelings are not fake.

Live Performance

Questioning

"Like water and fire... from an artistic perspective, it possesses strong contrast and dramatic tension."

This performance combines projection installations, dance, and motion graphics to construct the mental landscape of a patient with bipolar disorder.

I designed a storyline where a girl with bipolar disorder engages in a dialogue with a doctor. However, the doctor's endless questioning pushes her mental state into a more fragile condition.
